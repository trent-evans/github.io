Master of Software Development

As I mentioned above, I'm studying Software Development at the University of Utah. Specifically, I'm in the Master of Software Development program, a program that was launched two years ago as a way to introduce more software developers and engineers into the job market in Utah. I'm part of the 3rd cohort of students to enter the program. The program is specifically geared towards those with little to no background in computer science, so we have a very diverse group.

If you're curious to see some cool things I've done in the course, you can check out my portfolio. (It's empty right now, but I'm going to put a few things in it soon)

Long term, I'd like to get into software development geared towards medical device design. My undergraduate degree is in Biomedical Engineering which is what drives me to apply the things I'm learning in software development to help build up better and more customized medical devices and interfaces to help with patient needs around the globe.

I'm also very interested in studying more about Biomedical Informatics and I'm considering pursuing their graduate certificate after I earn my MSD.
